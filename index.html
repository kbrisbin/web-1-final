About


I’m Katrina - lover of life, food & fur babies! I'm self-taught photographer who was able to turn my passion into my occupation. I have a sincere passion for all things design, beautiful light, fresh food, sincere people and beautiful spaces. My military work allowed me to travel often and is a direct reflection of the people, stories and places that inspire me. When I’m not behind the lens, you can find me roaming the halls of the Catalyst Building in Spokane, WA. There I study Design and generally get to hang out with pretty cool people, just like you!
